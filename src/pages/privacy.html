Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

What We Collect

Stripe Auditor only accesses your Stripe account data during the audit process. Stripe secret keys are used in real-time and are not stored on our servers. We do not collect personal information unless you contact us via email.

How We Use Data

Audit reports are generated from the data you authorize us to access via your Stripe secret key. This data is used solely to generate your audit report and is not shared with third parties.

Data Retention

Free audit data is not retained after the report is generated. Subscribed accounts retain audit results for the duration of their subscription for comparison purposes. You may request deletion at any time.
